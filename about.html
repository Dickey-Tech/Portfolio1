Passionate About Electrical Innovation

I design and develop practical solutions across power systems, industrial control panels, embedded motor control, and renewable energy integration—turning ideas into dependable engineering.

My work spans from low-level circuit thinking (signal integrity, stable control logic, safe power handling) to higher-level system design such as distribution and monitoring strategies that keep installations predictable.

I enjoy mentoring aspiring engineers and collaborating on real-world prototypes where performance, reliability, and clean documentation matter.
